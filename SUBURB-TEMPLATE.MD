## Tone Calibration by Suburb Type

| Suburb type | Tone adjustment |
|-------------|----------------|
| **Bendigo** | Home turf — most confident, most specific, name local landmarks if relevant |
| **Ballarat, Shepparton, Geelong** | Regional peer cities — peer-to-peer, "we understand your market" |
| **Small regional (Horsham, Mildura, Sale)** | Emphasise isolation + opportunity: "you have almost no digital competition yet" |
| **Melbourne fringe (Dandenong, Frankston)** | More competitive framing — city tactics, regional understanding |
| **Melbourne inner** | Standard competitive framing — less "regional" angle, more "results" angle |

## AHPRA Compliance Reminders

- ✅ "Clinics we work with have seen growth in bookings" — OK
- ✅ "10 new patients per month" with disclaimer about results varying — OK
- ✅ "More visibility in local search" — OK
- ❌ "We guarantee 40 new patients" — NOT OK
- ❌ "The best dental marketing in [Suburb]" — NOT OK
- ❌ Any testimonial referencing a patient health outcome — NOT OK

When uncertain → flag for `au-legal-compliance-agent` before publishing.
